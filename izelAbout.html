History

Izel represents, Truth, being able to be free in spirit and show how the cultures holds tradition. We have named our product after the Mayan god. Ix Chel the goddess of the moon, she of the rainbow. Ix Chel is both male and female. This is how our product "Izel" pretains to these gods, we wanted fashion that is unisex. Something that anybody could wear to exress themselves.
